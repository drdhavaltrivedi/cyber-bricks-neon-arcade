# Privacy Policy - Cyber-Bricks: Neon Arcade

**Effective Date:** June 3, 2026

At **Cyber-Bricks: Neon Arcade** (referred to as "we", "us", or "the extension"), we respect and protect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding user information for our browser extension game and landing website.

---

## 🔒 1. Browser Extension Data Policy

The Cyber-Bricks: Neon Arcade browser extension is designed to run **100% locally** inside your web browser.

### 🚫 No Data Collection
- We **do not collect, store, transmit, or share** any personal information, browsing history, user credentials, IP addresses, or device diagnostics.
- The extension does not contain any third-party analytics trackers, cookies, advertising trackers, or beacons.

### 💾 Local Data Storage
- The extension uses standard browser storage APIs (`chrome.storage.local` or `localStorage`) solely to save your local high scores, game progression (unlocked levels), and audio settings (volume and mute status).
- This data remains strictly on your local device and is never uploaded to any remote server or third party. You can clear this data at any time by resetting your browser storage or uninstalling the extension.

### 🌐 No Network Communications
- The extension requires **no remote network permissions** and makes no external network requests during gameplay. All logic, rendering, and sound synthesizers are bundled locally within the extension files.

---

## 💻 2. Landing Website Policy (Vercel Hosted)

If you visit our promotional landing website:

- **Static Hosting**: The landing site is hosted as a static page on Vercel. We do not use database integrations or user account creation.
- **Web Analytics**: We do not include tracking or analytics integrations. Standard server-side delivery logs generated by Vercel (such as request timestamps and file transfer sizes) are managed according to Vercel's standard security policies and are not linked to identifiable personal information.

## ⚙️ 3. Permissions Requested and Rationale

The extension requests the following permissions upon installation:
- **`storage`**: Used to save settings preferences (sound mute, sound volume, impact shake) and high scores locally on your machine.
